Say It Out Loud

"Okay, what's the catch?"

Fair question. Cheap things usually hide something. Here is everything, plainly.

Is ₹8,000 really the full price?
Yes. The only extra is your domain name, roughly ₹800 a year, paid to the registrar, not to us.
Do I pay anything monthly?
No. Hosting is free. An optional maintenance plan from ₹6,000 a year exists only if you want us on call.
So what IS the catch?
Volume. We build many sites, fast, from researched trade templates, so each one costs less. That is the entire trick.
What if I do not like something?
You are on the call while it is built. You point, we change it, in the moment.
My trade is not in the 34?
We still build it. The templates are head starts, not limits.
Can it grow later?
Yes. Start at ₹8,000, add booking, a blog, even full e-commerce later. Tiers are an upgrade path, not walls.
